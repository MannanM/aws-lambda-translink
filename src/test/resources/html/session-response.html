About your request

There was a problem with your information:

  • You can report an incorrect fare within 120 days of the occurrence and an investigation will occur to validate your claim.
  • Adjustments can only be processed when a fixed fare is charged. For example, if you touch on and forget to touch off, adjustments cannot be processed until after you have used your card again.
  • Your request must be for a single journey, this may include multiple trips if they were travelled within the same timeframe.
